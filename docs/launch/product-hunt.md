## Product name

**ChatGPT.cheap**

## Tagline (60 chars max)

`AEO monitoring for SMBs — know when ChatGPT recommends you`

Alternatives:
- `See how ChatGPT and Gemini answer about your brand`
- `Track your brand in AI answers — from $9/month`
- `AEO monitoring for freelancers, priced for SMB`

## Description (short, 260 chars max for PH body)

Enterprise AEO tools cost $500/mo. ChatGPT.cheap monitors how ChatGPT and Gemini talk about your brand for $9. Weekly reports, sentiment, competitor radar, email alerts when your mention rate changes. For solo founders and SMBs who can’t afford Profound or Peec.

## Maker’s first comment (critical — write this ahead of time)

```
Hey Product Hunt 👋

I’m the solo maker behind ChatGPT.cheap. Here’s why I built it.

Last month I was chatting with a freelancer who makes Notion templates. He asked ChatGPT “what are the best Notion templates for startup founders” — his template wasn’t mentioned. His competitor’s was, at position 1. He didn’t know how long that had been true, and he had no way to monitor it without paying $500/mo for Profound.

I looked around. Every AEO tool is priced for enterprise. The actual long-tail of people who would benefit most — solo founders, freelancers, SMBs, personal brands — have nothing.

So I built this. $9/month gets you:
- 5 queries across ChatGPT + Gemini, with live web search on both
- Weekly automated runs + email alerts when your mention rate changes
- Sentiment analysis, list position, competitor detection on every result
- Full raw responses so you can see exactly what the AI says

The margin math only works because I cut everything non-essential: no free tier, no team seats, no integrations, no Claude yet. Just the core thing, done right.

Would love your feedback — especially if you spot the “but what if” cases I missed. I’ll be in the comments all day.

— [your name]
```

## Launch day timing

- Go live at 00:01 PT (tech audience is US-heavy)
- Post link to X, Reddit, relevant Slack/Discord communities within the first hour
- Stay online 6–8h for comment replies

## First 10 comments to respond to

Pre-draft short honest replies for the most common questions:
- "How does this differ from Semrush/SurferSEO?" — different problem: SEO measures Google rankings, AEO measures AI answer appearances
- "Will you add Claude?" — yes, on roadmap (month 2)
- "Can I see it without signing up?" — yes, screenshots + blog post link
- "Is there a free trial?" — no free tier by design (see blog post), 14-day refund window

## What NOT to do

- Do not buy upvotes or use bots. PH will flag and delist.
- Do not launch on a Monday or Friday (lower tech traffic).
- Do not launch without the pipeline actually running in production against real brands.
